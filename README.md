### OPERATION

* W to hard drop
* S to soft drop
* A to move left
* D to move right
* ->(right) to spin clockwise
* <-(left) to spin counterclockwise
* up to spin 180 degree
* Space to HOLD
* R to restart the game
* P to pause the gam (only works when piece is not yet dropped)
